About Me

Welcome! I am an undergraduate student pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Geography, Population, and Environmental Studies at the University of Nairobi. My academic and technical focus centers on Geographic Information Systems (GIS), Remote Sensing, and spatial analysis workflows.

Through my coursework, I have gained hands-on experience dealing with hydrology, regional development challenges in Arid and Semi-Arid Lands (ASALs), and quantitative data analysis methodologies. This portfolio highlights my practical mapping applications and spatial visualization designs.

Featured Project: Samburu County Educational Institutions

This layout uses ArcMap to categorize educational institutions across Samburu County by level—distinguishing between primary schools, secondary schools, colleges, and universities. It maps their regional distribution against localized coordinate projections to evaluate infrastructure access.
